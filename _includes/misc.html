I am originally from 西安 (Xi'an), a city with long history, in north-central China. Xi'an, also know as 长安 (Chang'an) in history, was the capital of thirteen Chinese dynasties. There are many historical sites, including the Terracotta Army and the starting point of the Silk Road.

Xi'an is also famous for its diverse and delicious wheat-based food. Making from scratch and enjoying hometown food is one of the most fulfilling activities for me, both mentally and physically.
